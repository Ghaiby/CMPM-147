Description

In this expirament, we're building a generator that directly reads black and white high contrast images, renders parameterized images, and adjusts its own designs to optimize an evaluation score.

Technical

  1. This project integrates images of an eye, a pier, and a flower that are selectable from the drop-down on the display page.
  2. The design is based on the colors of the pixels in the images. Images with different shapes will be rendered with different shaes .
  3. The designs resemble the images after being mutated properly (30s trick)
  4. The rate parameter allows the user to almost stop exploration by sliding the rate down to 1%.

Annealing

I think that the tip to start at 100% and slowly go to 1 over 30 seconds really helped see how well the design is doing and I was able to use it to get results similar ot the inspiration.

Reflection

I was confused on what this experiment was doing in the beggining, But after playing around and reading the directions closely was able to get nice results
